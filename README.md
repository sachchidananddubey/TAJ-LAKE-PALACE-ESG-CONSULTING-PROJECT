# 🏨 ESG Consulting Strategy for Taj Lake Palace, Udaipur

> Business Consulting | Sustainability | ESG Strategy | Hospitality | Cost-Benefit Analysis

This project presents a comprehensive ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) consulting strategy developed for **Taj Lake Palace, Udaipur**.

The objective was to identify critical operational challenges affecting sustainability, guest experience, and governance while proposing practical, financially viable, and scalable solutions.

The recommendations were developed using business analysis frameworks, feasibility studies, customer review analysis, implementation planning, and cost-benefit analysis.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

The study identified challenges across all three ESG dimensions.

### Environmental

- Plastic waste accumulation
- Metal waste generation
- Inefficient recycling practices

### Social

- Poor guest experience
- Staff behaviour issues
- Weak complaint handling process
- Lack of structured customer feedback

### Governance

- Infrastructure maintenance issues
- Governance gaps
- Theft prevention challenges
- Lack of operational monitoring
- Limited performance evaluation systems

# Key Recommendations

## 1. Sustainable Waste Management

Develop a circular economy model by converting plastic and metal waste into:

- Souvenirs
- Keychains
- Coasters
- Decorative products

### Benefits

- Waste reduction
- Additional revenue stream
- Community employment
- Brand enhancement

---

## 2. Customer Feedback System

Proposed a digital feedback ecosystem including:

- QR-based feedback forms
- Email feedback collection
- Automated responses
- Personalized grievance handling
- Internal analytics dashboard

---

## 3. Staff Performance Management

Recommended:

- Staff segmentation
- Dedicated feedback team
- Mystery shopper program
- 360° performance appraisal
- Recognition & reward system

---

## 4. Governance Improvements

Suggested:

- Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
- Risk management framework
- Property Management System integration
- Operational dashboards
- Transparency and accountability mechanisms

---

## 5. Infrastructure Improvements

Addressed pigeon infestation using:

- Bird netting
- Ultrasonic devices
- Chemical repellents

along with comparative implementation costs.

---

## 6. Theft Prevention Strategy

Proposed multiple security technologies:

- RFID Tags
- Invisible Ink
- Microdots
- CCTV enhancement
- Employee screening
- Guest awareness initiatives

A detailed cost-benefit analysis estimated a payback period of approximately **1.08 years**.
